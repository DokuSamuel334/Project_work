Our Study Group

We are a diverse group of Information Technology students from the School of Science, united by our passion for technology and innovation. Our group consists of 10 dedicated students from various academic levels, each bringing unique perspectives and skills to our collaborative learning environment.

Founded in 2024, our study group focuses on peer-to-peer learning, project collaboration, and academic support. We regularly meet to discuss coursework, work on coding projects, and prepare for examinations together.
