About Our Network

The Student Group Network is more than just a study group - we're a community of learners who believe in the power of collaboration and shared knowledge. Our network spans across different departments within the School of Science, with a strong focus on information Technology and related fields.

Our Mission

To create an inclusive learning environment where students can grow academically, develop professionally, and build lasting friendships. We strive to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application through hands-on projects and real-world problem-solving.

What We Do

  • Weekly study sessions and group discussions
  • Collaborative coding projects and hackathons
  • Peer tutoring and academic support
  • Industry networking events and guest lectures
  • Social activities and team-building exercises
